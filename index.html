1. Why ESG investment

Investors are increasingly aware that a critical factor in any company’s performance is its approach to environmental, social, and governance (ESG) matters. Leading LPs and other investors are proactively and publicly making data-driven ESG strategies central to their investment philosophies.

Building ESG initiatives into your portfolio strategy is not a tick-the-box exercise; it’s a paradigm shift as investors seek to achieve both financial and non-financial returns from more sustainable and socially responsible investing.

2. Why us

Our ESG consultants work with investors of all types, including private equity firms, sovereign wealth funds, investment funds, and family offices, to help you embed ESG strategy into every decision, from due diligence to post-acquisition value creation planning.

Our broad expertise across all facets of Sustainability and Responsibility provide you with the full context you need to craft the optimal ESG investment strategy.
